AEGIS - Anonymiser - Exposing your data only the way you want

The Anonymiser makes it easier than ever to defined how other users can access your data.
It allows you to defined what data should be exposed to third parties and how they should be transformed to ensure the anonymity of the exposed dataset - basically allowing you to expose part of your data in a meaningful way, while ensuring details of your dataset can easily be hidden.
